## Completed

### Second Opinion Finder Audit
- **Goal:** Evaluate the standalone quiz tool for potential integration into the HRA website as a lead generation instrument and ad landing page
- **Audit document:** `Second-Opinion-Finder-Audit.md` (gitignored — not part of the build)
- **Key findings:**
  - Quiz concept and scoring logic are strong — good lead gen potential
  - Form does not actually submit anywhere (leads are lost on tab close)
  - Scheduling link points to Acuity (`highridgeadvisory.as.me`) — site uses TidyCal
  - Design inconsistencies: wrong body font (DM Sans vs Inter), slightly different navy/gold hex values, inline CSS vs shared stylesheet, minimal header/footer vs full site navigation
  - No analytics, no spam protection, no UTM tracking
  - Filename has spaces and parentheses — not viable for ad campaign URLs
- **Phased integration plan proposed:**
  1. Fix broken functionality and align design with main site
  2. Wire form submission to Netlify Forms + Netlify Function to email James
  3. Add GA4 analytics, UTM capture, cookie consent
  4. Place as standalone ad landing page (`/second-opinion`) + embedded CTAs on key site pages
- **MailerLite note:** James does not currently have a MailerLite account. Email marketing integration is pending his decision — not included in the current plan but can be added later
- **Status:** Awaiting approval from Kyle and James before any implementation begins
